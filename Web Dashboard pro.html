Water Wastage

The water waste produced by a community of people is commonly referred to as sewage. Wastewater that originates from toilets or activities such as draining kitchen sinks can be considered household or other buildings wastewater.

Toilets as Garbage cans

Regrettably, irresponsible individuals have deposited waste materials such as papers, sanitary products, and even some plastics that can be flushed into toilets.

Fats in cooking

The walls of the pipes are quickly covered by these materials, which reduce the diameter of the pipe system that drains wastewater into the sewer.

Improper Wastewater Handling

The release of harmful toxins that interfere with the normal activities of sea life results in the death of aquatic life.
